蓝牙连接帮助

第一步

在 GR III 设定中开启 Wi-Fi，确保 GR 显示 Wi-Fi标志：


第二步

在 GR III 设定中开启蓝牙:


开启后屏幕上会显示一个灰色的蓝牙标志:


第三步

在 GR III 蓝牙设定中 配对 - 执行配对,即进入等待配对状态:


最后

在 GRLover 中开始进行蓝牙连接，会自动搜索 GR III，第一次配对输入 GR III 屏幕上显示的配对码即可。如果您在GR III 解除已经配对的设备，您同样需要在iPhone 蓝牙设定中，将已经配对的设备忽略掉，这很重要。通常您无需解除已经配对的设备。


BlueTooth Connect Help

First step

Turn on Wi-Fi in GR III settings and make sure GR displays the Wi-Fi logo:


Second step

Turn on Bluetooth in GR III settings,Select [On anytime] or [On when power is on]:


After turning on, a gray Bluetooth logo will be displayed on the screen:


third step

Pairing in GR III Bluetooth settings-Perform pairing,The camera enters a pairing standby state, and the camera’s [Device Name] and [Pass- word] are displayed.


At last

After starting the Bluetooth connection in GRLover, it will automatically search for GR III. For the first pairing, enter the pairing code displayed on the GR III screen. If you unpair a paired device in GR III, you also need to ignore the paired device in the iPhone Bluetooth settings. This is very important. Normally you do not need to unpair a device that has already been paired.
