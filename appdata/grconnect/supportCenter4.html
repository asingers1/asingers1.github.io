蓝牙连接帮助

第一步

在GR III 设定中开启Wi-Fi

第二步

在GR III 设定中开启蓝牙，开启后屏幕上会显示一个灰色的蓝牙标志。

第三步

在GR III 蓝牙设定中 配对 - 执行配对

最后

在GRLover 中开始进行蓝牙连接，第一次配对输入配对码即可。如果您在GR III 解除已经配对的设备，您同样需要在iPhone 蓝牙设定中，将已经配对的设备忽略掉，这很重要。通常您无需解除已经配对的设备。

Bluetooth Connection Help

Step 1

Open Wi-Fi in GR III settings

Step 2

Turn on Bluetooth in GR III settings, and a gray Bluetooth logo will appear on the screen after turning on.

Step 3

Pairing - Executing Pairing in GR III Bluetooth Settings

Finally

Bluetooth pairing is started in GRLover, and the first pairing input pairing code is enough. If you remove the matched device in GR III, you also need to ignore the matched device in the Bluetooth settings of the iPhone, which is very important. Usually you don't need to remove the matched device.
