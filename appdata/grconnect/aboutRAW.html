Instructions on saving RAW files and picture quality

  • After the RAW file is downloaded, you need to go to the RAW page to perform related operations.
  • After saving a RAW file to an album, the preview in the album is blurry because iOS does not support direct preview of other RAW file formats, here we refer to Ricoh DNG. But the image size resolution is still RAW, it just looks like the resolution is very low. At this point you can try editing with Lightroom on the iPhone, you will see a full resolution image, or direct Airdrop to your Mac.
  • Image quality compression in App settings will only compress the size of image files, such as 10M 3M, and does not compress image quality. This is to save device storage space and share it with other apps. Of course you can still choose to turn off this switch without compression.
