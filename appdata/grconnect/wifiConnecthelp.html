可能遇到的 Wi-Fi 连接问题

GR 是通过相机自身发出Wi-Fi 信号，手机连接到相机Wi-Fi 从而达到传输图片的效果。


在这一过程中难免会遇到相机Wi-Fi 信号不佳、信号延迟等问题，导致虽然已经连接成功，但刷新还是提示连接失败的问题。


有效尝试：

  1. 关闭相机，几秒钟后打开相机并开启 Wi-Fi
  2. 连接前先断开您原有的 Wi-Fi 连接，即下拉控制中心中使Wi-Fi 按钮置灰。这将临时断开手机与路由器 Wi-Fi 的连接。如图所示：



  1. 在手机设置——Wi-Fi 列表中忽略掉相机 Wi-Fi，并尝试重新连接。

感谢您的反馈，这将有助于App 做的更好。



Possible Wi-Fi connection problems

GR is to send Wi-Fi signal through the camera itself, and the phone connects to the camera Wi-Fi to transfer pictures.


In this process, it is inevitable to encounter problems such as poor camera Wi-Fi signal and signal delay, resulting in the problem that although the connection is successful, the refresh still prompts connection failure.


Effective attempts:

  1. Turn off the camera, turn it on after a few seconds and turn on Wi-Fi
  2. Disconnect from your original Wi-Fi connection before connecting, i.e., gray out the Wi-Fi button in the Control Center drop-down. This will temporarily disconnect the phone from the router Wi-Fi connection. As shown in the figure.


  1. Ignore the camera Wi-Fi in the phone Settings--Wi-Fi list and try to reconnect.
